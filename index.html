Izuku Midoriya

Izuku Midoriya, also known as Deku, is the main protagonist of the My Hero Academia series. He is a young boy who dreams of becoming a hero despite being born without powers.

Katsuki Bakugo

Katsuki Bakugo, also known as Kacchan, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School and one of Izuku Midoriya's closest friends. He has a powerful Quirk called Explosion.

Shoto Todoroki

Shoto Todoroki is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School and one of the strongest students in the class. He has a powerful Quirk called Half-Cold Half-Hot.

Dabi

Dabi is a villain and a member of the League of Villains. He has a powerful Quirk called Cremation.

Toshinori Yagi

Toshinori Yagi, also known as All Might, is the world's greatest hero and the mentor of Izuku Midoriya. He has a powerful Quirk called One For All.

Shigaraki Tomura

Shigaraki Tomura is the leader of the League of Villains and one of the main antagonists of the My Hero Academia series. He has a powerful Quirk called Decay.

Synopsis
Introduction

My Hero Academia is an anime television series about a boy who enrolls in a prestigious hero academy after gaining superpowers from the world's greatest hero

Chapter 1:
The Rise of Quirks

The story begins with the introduction of Quirks, superpowers that have become commonplace in society. We meet Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero like All Might.

Chapter 2
The Legend of All Might

Midoriya encounters All Might, the Symbol of Peace, who reveals his true identity and the nature of his Quirk, One For All. Midoriya learns he can inherit this power.

Chapter 3:
Paranormal Liberation War Arc

A massive confrontation between heroes and the Paranormal Liberation Front, leading to significant casualties and revelations about the true nature of heroism and villainy. This arc deepens the narrative by exploring the motivations and backgrounds of both heroes and villains.
